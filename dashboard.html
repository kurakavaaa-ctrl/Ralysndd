Help & FAQ

Quick answers about running your Ralysnd gateway.

Why does the root URL show another website?

That's intentional camouflage — it hides the gateway from casual scanners. Your panel lives at the /dash path.

I forgot my master key.

Open your D1 database console and query the kv_store table for the masterKey entry.

Where do I add users?

Go to the Users tab and click "Add User" to generate a dedicated subscription link.
